1 — Sign upPhone number + Touch ID. Flow creates a smart-contract account server-side. No wallet app, no seed phrase, no crypto knowledge.
2 — Join circleUser selects group size and weekly amount. The AI Matching Agent suggests compatible circles based on their financial profile.
3 — ContributeUSDC wrapped as cUSDC (ERC-7984). Encrypted amount deposited on-chain. Group members see ✓ only — never the number.
4 — Auto-debitForte cron pulls contributions each cycle. CoopWise sponsors gas. Members pay nothing and do nothing — it just works.
5 — Rotation turnFHE.requestDecryption reveals the winner's share to their wallet only. ACL ensures no one else — not even the organiser — can see the amount.
6 — Bank hitConfidential unwrap → USDC → Flutterwave / Paystack → local currency. Funds in the recipient's bank account within 30–60 min.
7 — Agent layerHealth Agent scores the circle. Treasury Agent logs the flow. Retention nudges sent if any member's pattern looks risky next cycle.

Step 1 — Flow Forte cron fires (Scheduled Transaction)CoopWise.cdc detects that member[i]'s rotation turn has arrived. Constructs a Flow Actions chain: VaultSource (pull from all member vaults) → Swapper (FLOW → USDC via IncrementFi) → VaultSink (deposit into CoopWiseVault.sol via Cross-VM EVM call). UniqueIdentifier tags the entire operation for full event traceability.
Step 2 — FHEVM accumulates encrypted contributionsEach member's cUSDC wraps into CoopWiseVault.sol. FHE.add(encryptedPool, memberContribution) runs entirely on ciphertext. The total pool balance is euint64 — no individual amount or aggregate is ever visible on-chain. FHE.allowThis grants the contract accumulation access only.
Step 3 — FHE.requestDecryption for winner onlyContract calls FHE.requestDecryption(poolAmount, callbackPayoutHandler.selector). ACL grants decryption rights exclusively to the recipient address via FHE.allow(encryptedAmount, winnerAddress). Zama relayer SDK submits decryptionProof. callbackPayoutHandler fires with cleartext amount — no one else ever sees it.
Step 4 — Confidential unwrap + fiat offrampwrapper.unwrap(winner, offrampAddress, encryptedAmount). After finalizeUnwrap(burntAmount, cleartext, proof), USDC is released to CoopWise's offramp wallet. Flutterwave or Paystack initiates a bank transfer to the winner's account. NGN, KES, or GHS arrives within 30–60 minutes.
Step 5 — Treasury Agent logs & reconcilesThe Treasury Optimisation Agent receives the UnwrapFinalized event, logs the payout to the audit trail, updates its liquidity forecast model, and generates an updated circle summary for the admin dashboard. No autonomous reallocation — full read-only audit authority only.
